The Project:

Arpeggio is a music search database that allows users to search for songs or albums, either by title or by artist name.

After searching, the user can save songs/albums if logged in, view specific details about an item, and filter their results using the variety of methods listed below.

  • Genre
  • Award
  • Length
  • Released Before Date
  • Released After Date
  • Word(s) in song lyrics
